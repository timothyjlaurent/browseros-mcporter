# You.com Conversation Search (manual)

## Goal
Find old conversations in You.com and extract their content.

## Steps
1. Open https://you.com with BrowserOS.
2. Open the left sidebar (history). If hidden, use the menu icon.
3. Use the search field (if available) to filter past chats by keyword.
4. Open the desired conversation and extract the relevant messages.

## Notes
- If there is no search field, scroll the history list and open the closest match.
- Do not delete or rename conversations unless explicitly asked.
